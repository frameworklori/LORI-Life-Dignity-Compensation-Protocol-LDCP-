LORI-Life Dignity Compensation Protocol (LDCP)

生命尊嚴賠償協議（中英對照）

© 2025 LORI Ethical System – All Rights Reserved

⸻

Abstract｜摘要

EN
Modern societies rely on monetary compensation to address losses, yet human life itself cannot be priced by market logic. Traditional cost-benefit analysis often fails to capture dignity, grief, and moral responsibility. The LDCP establishes a four-stage, dignity-first compensation protocol that prioritizes moral accountability before economic calculation, restoring public trust and ensuring equitable justice.

中
現代社會雖然必須以金錢進行賠償，但人類生命本身並不能以市場邏輯定價。傳統的成本效益分析往往忽略尊嚴、悲痛與道德責任。LDCP 提出四階段的「尊嚴優先」賠償流程，將道德責任置於經濟計算之前，以重建社會信任並確保真正的正義。

⸻

I. Core Principles｜核心理念

1. Life Cannot Be Priced｜生命不可定價

EN
Compensation concerns losses—not the monetary value of a life.
中
賠償處理的是「損失」，而不是給生命「標價」。

⸻

2. Moral Duty Precedes Monetary Duty｜道德義務先於金錢義務

EN
Acknowledging harm and offering apology must come before financial settlement.
中
承認錯誤與致歉必須先於金錢和解。

⸻

3. Victims’ Families Come First, Not Corporate Cost Minimization｜以家屬為中心，而非企業成本

EN
Compensation should reflect the real dependency and impact on the family, not corporate profit calculations.
中
賠償應反映家庭的實際依賴與衝擊，而非企業利益最小化邏輯。

⸻

4. Transparent, Sequential, Predictable Procedure｜透明、有序、可預測的流程

EN
Each stage carries ethical significance and cannot be skipped or rearranged arbitrarily.
中
每一階段都有倫理目的，不能被跳過或任意調換。

⸻

II. Four-Stage Compensation Model｜四階段賠償模型

⸻

Stage 1 – Immediate Dignity Response

階段一：即時尊嚴處理

EN
Provide non-liability assistance payments such as condolence funds, funeral costs, and emergency support. This stage acknowledges suffering without involving liability assessment.
中
提供「非責任性款項」：慰問金、喪葬費、緊急協助等。此階段不討論責任，只為承認苦痛、維護尊嚴。

Purpose｜目的
	•	EN: Human-centered dignity recognition.
	•	中：以人性為核心，優先維護尊嚴。

⸻

Stage 2 – Moral Accountability & Apology

階段二：道德責任與正式致歉

EN
The company must provide a transparent explanation, acknowledge wrongdoing (when facts are clear), and meet with the family for a formal apology.
中
企業需公開說明事件、在事實明確時承認錯誤，並與家屬會面進行正式致歉。

Purpose｜目的
	•	EN: Repair trust beyond monetary value.
	•	中：修復金錢無法補償的信任。

⸻

Stage 3 – Safety Commitments & Prevention Plan

階段三：未來安全承諾與預防再發

EN
The company must present concrete improvements: design changes, safety protocols, risk models, quality upgrades, and prevention measures.
中
企業需提出具體改善措施：設計升級、風險模型、品質管控強化、再發防止機制等。

Purpose｜目的
	•	EN: Justice must protect future lives, not only compensate past harm.
	•	中：正義應保障未來生命，而非只補償過去損害。

⸻

Stage 4 – Full Compensation Based on Family Loss

階段四：依家庭損失的正式賠償

EN
Compensation must NOT be based on company cost-benefit analysis.
It must be based on:
	•	victim’s role as breadwinner
	•	age and projected future income
	•	dependent family members
	•	long-term economic impact
	•	emotional & psychological harm

中
賠償 不得 以企業成本效益為基準，而應依照：
	•	受害人是否為家中經濟支柱
	•	年齡與未來收入預測
	•	家庭依賴人口
	•	長期經濟衝擊
	•	精神與心理損害

Purpose｜目的
	•	EN: Reflect true family loss, not corporate convenience.
	•	中：反映家庭的真實損失，而非企業的便利計算。

⸻

III. Philosophical Connection to Sandel｜哲學對應：Sandel 的思路

EN
The LDCP aligns with Sandel’s critique of market overreach:
	•	not all values are commensurable,
	•	life cannot be reduced to monetary units,
	•	justice requires moral reasoning, not economic optimization.

中
LDCP 與 Sandel 對「市場邏輯入侵」的批判一致：
	•	價值並非都能用一把尺量化
	•	生命不能被換算為金錢
	•	正義需要道德思考，而非經濟最佳化

但 LDCP 更進一步提供 可執行的流程，讓哲學落地。

⸻

IV. Application Scenarios｜應用場景

EN
Industrial accidents, autonomous driving fatalities, aviation incidents, medical malpractice, food safety crises, public infrastructure failures.
中
工安意外、自動駕駛事故、航空事故、醫療疏失、食安事件、公共設施事故等。

⸻

V. Closing Statement｜結語

EN
The LDCP restores dignity by enforcing a moral-first sequence:
	1.	Dignity
	2.	Accountability
	3.	Safety
	4.	Compensation

中
LDCP 以「道德優先」重建生命尊嚴：
	1.	尊嚴
	2.	承認責任
	3.	未來安全
	4.	最終賠償—immediate support, moral accountability, safety improvement, and full family-based compensation—to ensure justice beyond cost-benefit calculations.  中 以尊嚴為先的四階段賠償流程：即時援助、道德承擔、安全改善、家庭損失賠償，避免以成本效益衡量生命，確保真正的正義。
