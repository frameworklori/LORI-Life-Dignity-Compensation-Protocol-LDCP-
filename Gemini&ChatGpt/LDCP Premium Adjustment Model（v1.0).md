#  LDCP Premium Adjustment Model v1.0

Life Dignity Compensation Protocol — Ethical Premium Adjustment Framework

⸻

1. Overview

The LDCP Premium Adjustment Model defines how insurance premiums are calculated when a company implements LDCP ethical standards, precision recall mechanisms, and safety transparency practices.
This model clarifies the difference between:
	•	Base Premium (不可改動的基數)
	•	Technical Risk Factors (技術風險)
	•	Ethical/Safety Adjustment Factor (道德與安全折扣因子)

This creates a fair pricing framework where transparency, responsibility, and precision safety reduce future risk and grant legitimate premium discounts.

⸻

2. Three-Layer Pricing Structure

Layer 1 — Base Premium (基數保費)

The base premium is calculated through traditional actuarial methods, including:
	•	Vehicle or product value
	•	Standard mortality/morbidity tables
	•	Historical claim frequency
	•	Coverage limits
	•	Traditional demographic factors

Characteristics:
	•	Cannot be negotiated
	•	Cannot be discounted by behavior alone
	•	Represents the fundamental financial exposure
  
  Formula

  Base Premium = Insured Value × Standard Risk Rate

  Layer 2 — Technical Risk Factor (技術風險係數)

This layer measures the probability of technical failure or defect.

Includes:
	•	IoT malfunction data
	•	Production batch variability
	•	Component failure rates
	•	Predictive maintenance signals
	•	AI-based early warning systems
	•	Precision recall readiness

Typical range:
Technical Risk Factor = 0.7 to 1.5

Lower value = safer product.
Higher value = higher failure probability.

⸻

Layer 3 — Ethical/Safety Adjustment Factor (道德與安全折扣因子)

This is the LDCP innovation.

This layer evaluates company behavior, not hardware risk.
It quantifies:
	•	Ethical transparency
	•	Timely apology and responsibility
	•	LDCP Stage 1–4 compliance
	•	Precision recall execution capability
	•	Willingness to cooperate with IAP (Independent Assessment Panel)
	•	Historical integrity performance
	•	Safety improvement commitments
	•	Public and family communication quality

The result becomes a measurable discount or surcharge.

Final Premium = Base Premium × Technical Risk Factor × Ethical/Safety Adjustment ⭐ 3. Why This Model Does NOT Replace Base Premium

Because LDCP:
	•	Does not insure financial loss
	•	Does not replace actuarial methods
	•	Does not price the value of life
	•	Only reduces future risk through ethical behavior and precision recall

Therefore, LDCP affects Adjustments, not Base Value.

(Base Premium)

The first layer (Base Premium) is the only part based on “基數”.
The second and third layers represent 企業行為 & 實際風險降低, not基數。

⭐ 中文版（對照版）

LDCP 保費調整模型 v1.0

生命尊嚴賠償協議 — 保費三層級定價架構

⸻

1. 概述

LDCP 保費模型定義企業在採用 LDCP 道德標準、精準召回、透明化安全流程後，保費該如何計算。
此模型清楚區分：
	•	基數保費（基礎不可變）
	•	技術風險係數
	•	道德與安全折扣因子

目的：
將「企業的道德行為」轉換成 可量化保費折扣，不是取代基數。

⸻

2. 保費三層架構

第一層：基數保費（Base Premium）

由傳統精算方法計算：
	•	車輛/產品價值
	•	傳統死亡率、事故率
	•	歷史理賠數據
	•	保額大小

特性：
	•	不可協商
	•	不可受到行為影響
	•	為保險公司承擔的基本風險範圍

公式：
基數保費 = 保額 × 標準風險率

第二層：技術風險係數（Technical Risk Factor）

包含：
	•	IoT 故障紀錄
	•	零件批次差異
	•	預測性維修訊號
	•	精準召回能力
	•	AI 故障預測

值域：
0.7 ～ 1.5

第三層：道德與安全折扣因子（Ethical/Safety Adjustment Factor）

LDCP 模組的創新要素。

評估：
	•	道德透明度
	•	事故後的正式道歉與承擔
	•	是否執行 LDCP 1–4 階段
	•	精準召回速度
	•	是否合作 IAP
	•	企業誠信紀錄
	•	家屬溝通質量
	•	安全改進落實程度

⭐ 結論一句話

**基數決定保費的起點；

道德與安全決定保費的折扣。**

這是 LDCP 的核心價值。
