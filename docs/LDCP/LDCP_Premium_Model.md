# ENGLISH VERSION (Top)

## 0. Overview
The LDCP Premium Model converts moral behavior, transparency, and safety improvements into insurance discount factors.  
This overturns the traditional system where companies hide failures to avoid cost.

---

# 1. Structure of the Premium Model

Final Premium
= Base Premium
	•	Technical Risk Adjustment
– LDCP Moral–Safety Adjustment

---

# 2. Base Premium Layer
Determined by:
- Production volume exposure  
- Per-unit accident rates  
- Vehicle structural safety  
- Historical performance  

---

# 3. Technical Risk Adjustment
Based on:
- Defect rate  
- Recall frequency  
- Autopilot/ADAS failure probability  
- Software reliability  
- Component failure rate  

---

# 4. LDCP Moral–Safety Adjustment
Three discount categories:

1. Moral Accountability  
2. Transparency  
3. Precision Recall  

LDCP reduces **uncertainty**, the most expensive risk element in actuarial science.

---

# 5. Formula (Simplified)
LDCP Adjustment
= f(MoralScore, TransparencyScore, RecallAccuracyScore)
× RiskPredictabilityWeight
Max discounts:
- Moral: −30%  
- Transparency: −10%  
- Precision Recall: −15%

---

# 6. Value to All Parties
- OEM: lower unit insurance cost  
- Drivers: safer, more transparent ownership  
- Insurers: stable, predictable risk

# 中文版本 (Bottom)

## 0. 概述
LDCP 保費模型把道德行為、透明度、安全改善轉換成保費折扣因子。  
它推翻傳統「隱瞞才能省錢」的制度，使車廠負責任反而更便宜。

---

# 1. 保費模型結構

最終保費
= 基礎保費
	•	技術風險調整
– LDCP 道德安全折扣

---

# 2. 基礎保費
由以下決定：
- 銷售量（暴露量）  
- 單位事故率  
- 結構性安全  
- 歷史記錄  

---

# 3. 技術風險調整
依據：
- 缺陷率  
- 召回頻率  
- 自動駕駛錯誤率  
- 軟體穩定度  
- 零件壽命  

---

# 4. LDCP 道德安全折扣
三種折扣來源：

1. 道德承擔  
2. 透明度  
3. 精準召回  

LDCP 大幅降低保險模型中最昂貴的變數：「不確定性」。

---

# 5. 公式（簡化）

LDCP 折扣
= f(道德分數, 透明分數, 精準召回分數)
× 可預測性權重
最大折扣：
- 道德：−30%  
- 透明：−10%  
- 精準召回：−15%

---

# 6. 三方共贏
- 車廠：單位保費下降  
- 駕駛：更安全、更透明  
- 保險公司：風險更可預測
