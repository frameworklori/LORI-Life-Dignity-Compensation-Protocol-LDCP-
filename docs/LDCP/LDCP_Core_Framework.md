# ENGLISH VERSION (Top)

## 0. Purpose
LDCP (Life Dignity Compensation Protocol) establishes a dignity-first, transparent, and accountable structure for managing automotive fatal incidents.  
It fixes the long-standing structural failures in the industry: delay, concealment, PR manipulation, and cost-first thinking.

LDCP aims to:
- Protect driver and family dignity  
- Standardize verifiable accident handling  
- Convert morality into financial stability  
- Eliminate incentives for concealment  
- Enable predictable insurance modeling  

---

# 1. Core Principles

### **1.1 Life Cannot Be Priced**
Compensation reflects family loss, not the value of life.

### **1.2 Moral Duty Before Monetary Duty**
Apology, responsibility, and truth-telling must occur before compensation negotiation.

### **1.3 Transparency Over PR**
Corporate image cannot override the right to truth.

### **1.4 Prevention Is the Real Justice**
Long-term safety improvement is more important than any payout.

---

# 2. Four LDCP Stages

## **Stage 1 — Immediate Dignity Response**
Humanitarian support, family contact, acknowledgment of the incident.

## **Stage 2 — Moral Accountability**
Public apology, executive responsibility, third-party verification.

## **Stage 3 — Safety Commitments**
Root-cause analysis, batch-code tracing, precision recall implementation.

## **Stage 4 — Compensation**
Independent assessment panel (IAP) calculates family-impact-based restitution.

---

# 3. Why LDCP Works
LDCP is the world’s first system to convert:
**ethics → transparency → predictable risk → lower insurance cost.**

---

# 4. System Architecture
LDCP =  
Ethics Layer → Safety Layer → Transparency Layer → Actuarial Layer → Compensation Layer

# 中文版本 (Bottom)

## 0. 目的
LDCP（生命尊嚴賠償協議）是一套以尊嚴為基礎、重視透明與問責的車業事故處理標準。  
它修正車業長期存在的結構性問題：延遲、隱瞞、公關包裝、以成本為優先。

LDCP 的目標：
- 保障駕駛與家屬的尊嚴  
- 建立可驗證、可審查的事故流程  
- 將道德行為轉換成財務穩定性  
- 取消隱瞞誘因  
- 讓保險更可預測  

---

# 1. 核心原則

### **1.1 生命不可定價**
賠償代表家庭損失，而不是生命的價格。

### **1.2 道德優先於金錢**
道歉、承擔責任、說出真相優先於金錢談判。

### **1.3 透明重於公關**
企業形象不能壓過事實。

### **1.4 預防才是真正的正義**
長期安全改善比任何金錢更重要。

---

# 2. LDCP 四階段模型

## **第一階段：尊嚴處理**
人道協助、聯繫家屬、承認事故。

## **第二階段：道德承擔**
公開道歉、高層負責、第三方調查。

## **第三階段：安全改善**
根因分析、批次追蹤、精準召回。

## **第四階段：賠償**
由 IAP 計算基於家庭損失的補償金額。

---

# 3. LDCP 為何有效
LDCP 是全球首個將：  
**倫理 → 透明 → 可預測風險 → 降低保費**  
整合成一個閉環系統的架構。

LDCP =  
倫理 → 安全 → 透明 → 精算 → 賠償
